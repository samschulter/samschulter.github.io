* Samuel Schulter
I'm a senior researcher in the [[http://www.nec-labs.com/research-departments/media-analytics/media-analytics-home][Media-Analytics Department at NEC-Labs America]] in Cupertino, CA.
My research interests are in computer vision and machine learning. I'm currently working on object detection and tracking in videos and higher-level 3D scene understanding.
I received my PhD in 2015 from Graz University of Technology under the supervision of Horst Bischof.
