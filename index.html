Samuel Schulter

Senior research scientist at NEC Laboratories America, Inc (NEC Laboratories America, Inc., Amazon). My research interests are in computer vision and machine learning.

Google Scholar - LinkedIn

I'm co-organizing a workshop at CVPR 2023 titled OmniLabel: Infinite label spaces for semantic understanding via natural language. Check out the website of the workshop at https://sites.google.com/view/omnilabel-workshop-cvpr23. More details on the new benchmark can be found at https://www.omnilabel.org and https://arxiv.org/abs/2304.11463
